Until Kingdom Come

i was walking home when she stopped me
she asked me where i was going i told her i didnt know, i was told to just walk

she showed me to her apartment and we planted sunflower seeds. time came for spring and they bloomed quite nice.

we held each other near the petals and we scrunched our noses close, until the wrinkles grew on her.

but the wrinkles only grew on her. My face, still babylike. soft and new. for then, at that moment, we knew.

she asked me where i was headed, and by the look of her face she knew where i was headed. i didnt answer. instead i sealed it inside her forehead, a quick kiss. she knew, we knew, and the sun had set.

the flowers will begin to wither, and the beds will flood with tomorrows dew. the fertilizer for the future of the light children.

Until kingdom come.
